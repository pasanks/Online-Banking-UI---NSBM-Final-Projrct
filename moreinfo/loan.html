Loan Facilities

This type of online loan is perfect when you need to consolidate debts. You will not need to back it up by any collateral, such as a car or house: you can get financing for whatever you need. Even financial coaches recommend personal loans for debt consolidation. Applying through Personal Money Service has never been easier.

These are personal loans ranging from amounts of US Doller 500 to US Doller 20,000 extended to salaried individuals with minimum net salaries of US Doller 250. No security is required for this loan.

Features
  • The loan duration is a minimum of 12 months to a maximum of 48 months.
  • Response time in 48 hours.
  • Maximum amount of the loan is US Doller 20,000 and a minimum of US Doller 500.
Benefits
  • Competitive interest rates.
  • Flexible interest installment matched to your income.
  • Quick processing and disbursement within one week.
  • Minimum loan amount US Doller 500.
Eligibility
  • Any salaried individual who has been in continuous employment for over 6 months.
  • Minimum net qualifying salary of US Doller 250.
  • Maximum qualifying age is 55 years.
  • Processing fee of 2%.
  • Clean Credit Reference Bureau report.
Requirements
  • Submission of duly completed BANK DE SEMICOLON Personal Loan application form.
  • Letter of undertaking from employer confirming employment and that salary shall be sent directly to the applicant’s BANK DE SEMICOLON account.
  • Copy of a valid ID.
  • The originals of the preceding 3 months’ payslips.
  • For non-BDS customers, certified copies of bank statements for the preceding 6 months.

We work with people who have various credit scores: from bad to fair, from fair to good. Do not assume that you can’t qualify for a loan because of a poor credit score situation. Just fill out our quick application and wait for your answer. The best part is that we will not bore you with the long application form and long hours of waiting.

Personal Money Service is the best online loan provider. We strongly recommend controlling your personal finances. Yet, if you need to close credit card debts, pay a bill or even fund a big expense, we are always at your services. Go to the application form and fill out the necessary information. Once the application is sent out, you should expect to get the loan decision once the application is submitted.
