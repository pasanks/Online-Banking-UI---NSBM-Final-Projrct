Saving Solutions

Whether you are just beginning to save or have some money built up, we have a variety of savings options to fit your needs at De Semicolon Bank, we believe it's important to save for both the big and small moments that com up in life. Our Certificates of Deposit help you do just that. Our Certificates of Deposits are best rated in last three years, with flexible minimum purchase amounts and rollover options.
